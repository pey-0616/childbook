Sophia Wakes Up

Sophia wakes up in bed as sunlight fills her room. She stretches and gets ready to start her day.

Sophia Meets the Animals

Sophia walks outside and sees a group of small animals playing in the park. She wants to join them, but feels a little shy.

Sophia Joins the Play

Sophia holds her favorite toy ball and walks over to the animals, inviting them to play.

The Ball Falls in the River

While playing, the ball accidentally rolls into the river. Sophia feels a bit scared, but she knows she needs to figure out how to get it back.

Sophia Retrieves the Ball

Sophia finds a long stick and carefully uses it to retrieve the ball from the river.

Sophia Shares the Ball

Sophia gives the ball to the smallest animal so it can play with them.

Sophia Gets Help

Sophia accidentally falls and hurts herself a little. Her friends immediately rush to help, gently picking her up and comforting her.

Sophia Thanks Her Friends

Sophia gratefully says to her friends, “Thank you for helping me, I feel much better!”

Playing Together

Sophia and her new friends happily play together, their laughter filling the park.

Sophia Says Goodbye

As the day comes to an end, Sophia says goodbye to her friends and heads home. Before falling asleep, she smiles as she remembers the day’s adventure.
